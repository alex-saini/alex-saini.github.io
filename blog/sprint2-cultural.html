My Learning Plan

Strengths and Limitations

My strengths are my loyalty, trustworthiness, motivation, empathy and compassion. My limitations are that I can get easily irritated by things that don't interest me, I can lose interest and get distracted by this too. I can keep things to myself when I don't feel as if they will contribute even though they probably could.

Workload

Being mindful and practicing mindfulness daily. Being mindful of peers and others around me, offering help and asking with discretion but openly. Keeping in a routine, waking up at a certain time, eating healthy, exercising, practicing work techniques such as pomodoro so I can timebox and work more effeciently. Using breathing exercises and taking breaks when scheduled to relieve stress and keep my mind fresh.

Seeking help

If I'm blocked with work, I will first look over what I'm doing, then head to the internet for answers. If still blocked I will head to students/peers/tutors for help when suits them and myself.

What I expect from the team

I expect a level of support with work, for the team to be understanding, helpful and give meaningful critisicm/tell me how to improve my work if I am not doing something properly.
