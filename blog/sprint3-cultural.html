Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is the aim of understanding our own emotions and understanding the emotions of others. It covers:

  • Self-Awareness: Understanding your impact.
  • Empathy: Empathizing based on reaction and also analyzing your empathy.
  • Social-Skills: Being able to manage change and conflict. Able to communicate and listen compassionately.
  • Motivation: Having drive for what you do.
  • Self-Regulation: Knowing your values, mindfulness, holding yourself accountable for your actions.


How is IQ different to EQ?

While IQ is the measure of your intelligence, EQ is the measure of what I listed above. IQ specifically is the focus on your cognative abilties. Some of these being:

  • Short-Term Memory
  • Analytical Thinking
  • Mathematical Ability
  • Spatial Recognition


Why is EQ important?

Within the tech workspace, teamwork is a fundamental part of completing tasks and working efficiently. Emotional Intelligence teaches and allows you to do so much better in these workspaces. Understanding, listening and communicating are all important aspects when working with a team.
